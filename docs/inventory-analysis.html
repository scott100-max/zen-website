Other Script Rules

Additional patterns that cause Fish/Marco TTS defects at generation time.

PatternExampleDefectFix
Opening chunk ≥60 chars“Today we are going to practise something that might seem uncomfortable at first...” (163ch) Cold-start artefact on first generationSplit into a short opener (<60ch) followed by the rest
Number as first word“First, name it.” / “Ten. Picture something...” Cold-start voice defectPrepend carrier phrase or merge with preceding chunk
Single letter as first word“S. Something silver.” Cold-start — nothing for Fish to anchor onMerge with preceding chunk
Isolated “be” at sentence end“...wherever you want to be.” Voice-shift / pitch drop at tailRewrite: “...wherever feels right for you.”
Hard/short closing word“...giving it a try.” / “...Day Six.” Fish truncates final generation — hard consonants get clippedEnd on soft multi-syllable word: “...tomorrow”, “...journey”
Parenthetical tags(softly), (gently), (breathing) Fish reads these aloud as literal textRemove entirely — use emotion parameter instead
“This is your...” opening“This is your seventh day.” Declarative tone sounds robotic in TTSInvitational opener: “Welcome to...” or scene-setting
