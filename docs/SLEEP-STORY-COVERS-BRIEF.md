- Portrait orientation (book cover proportions, roughly 3:4 ratio)
- "A SLEEP STORY" small text at top
- Title in elegant typography (mix of serif and script)
- Short tagline at bottom
- Rich, atmospheric imagery matching the story theme
- Save as PNG
- Filename: kebab-case of title (e.g. "The Night Train" → `the-night-train.png`)
- Save to: `content/images/sleep-stories/`
