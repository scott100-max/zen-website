15 min · Mindfulness

About This Session

Anchor your attention on the breath — the most fundamental mindfulness practice. Learn to follow each inhale and exhale with gentle curiosity, returning patiently each time your mind wanders.

What You'll Learn

  • Breath as an anchor for attention
  • The wandering mind is normal — not failure
  • Three styles of breath awareness
  • Building from 5 to 15 minutes of practice
Best for: Daily practice, building concentration, stress reduction
