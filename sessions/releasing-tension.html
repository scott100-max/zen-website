Releasing Tension

15 min · Stress
Narrated by Marco
← All Meditations

About This Session

A guided progressive muscle relaxation to physically release the stress you're carrying. Systematically tense and release each muscle group, teaching your body the difference between tension and relaxation.

What You'll Learn

  • Progressive muscle relaxation (PMR)
  • Identifying where you hold tension
  • The tension-release cycle
  • Using PMR as a daily stress management tool
Best for: Physical tension, headaches, jaw clenching, shoulder pain
