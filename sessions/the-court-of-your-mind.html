CBT Exercise

The Court of Your Mind

Your inner critic has been running the trial unchallenged. This session puts you in the courtroom — and for the first time, you hear the defence.

25 min Cognitive Restructuring Premium
Narrated by Marco
Scroll
← All Sessions

About This Session

Your inner critic has been building a case against you for years. It speaks with absolute certainty, predicting failure and inadequacy. But it has never once been cross-examined.

This CBT-inspired meditation places you in a courtroom where the prosecution (your self-doubt) makes its case and the defence asks one devastating question: where is your evidence? Through guided recall of your own real experiences, you discover that the prosecution's papers were always blank.

What You'll Practise

  • Identifying the inner critic as a voice, not a fact
  • Cross-examining negative self-talk with evidence
  • Recalling personal evidence of resilience and capability
  • Building a single question that dismantles self-doubt every time
Best for: Self-doubt, imposter syndrome, anxiety before meetings or challenges, rumination
