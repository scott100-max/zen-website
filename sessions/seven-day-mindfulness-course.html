Day 1: Breath Awareness

12 minutes · Breath awareness · Your foundation

Everything begins with the breath. Today you'll learn to use your breath as an anchor — a place to return to whenever your mind wanders. This single skill is the foundation of every mindfulness practice that follows.

0:00 / 0:00

Day 2: Body Scan

12 minutes · Body scan · Connecting mind and body

Learn to scan your body from head to toe, noticing sensations without trying to change them. This practice builds the interoceptive awareness that helps you recognise stress before it overwhelms you.

Unlock Full Course

Day 3: Noting Thoughts

12 minutes · Thought observation · Seeing the mind clearly

Thoughts are not facts. Today you'll practise labelling thoughts as they arise — "thinking", "planning", "worrying" — without getting caught up in their content. This gentle noting technique creates space between you and your mental chatter.

Unlock Full Course

Day 4: Emotional Awareness

12 minutes · Emotions · Feeling without reacting

Emotions live in the body. Today you'll learn to notice where emotions show up physically — the tightness, the warmth, the flutter — and simply observe them without needing to fix or flee.

Unlock Full Course

Day 5: Mindful Movement

12 minutes · Movement · Awareness in action

Mindfulness doesn't require sitting still. Today you'll bring awareness to gentle movement — feeling each motion, each shift in weight. This bridges formal practice and everyday life.

Unlock Full Course

Day 6: Loving-Kindness

12 minutes · Compassion · Opening the heart

Direct warmth and goodwill toward yourself and others. Loving-kindness practice has been shown to increase positive emotions, reduce self-criticism, and strengthen social connection.

Unlock Full Course

Day 7: Integration

12 minutes · Daily life · Making it last

Everything comes together. Today you'll weave all six practices into a single sitting, then explore how to carry mindfulness into your daily routine — walking, eating, working, and resting.
