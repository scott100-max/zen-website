Welcome to Salus

Sleep. Relax. Restore.

Life moves fast. Your mind doesn't have to. Salus is a meditation and mindfulness platform built to help you slow down, breathe, and reconnect with what matters most.

We're a family. Not a corporation. Scott has over 40 years of personal experience with mindfulness, meditation, cognitive behavioural therapy, counselling, breathwork, acupuncture, and ASMR. Our consultant holds a BSc (Hons) and MSc in Psychology. No expensive celebrities. No venture capital. No boardroom. We built Salus because these tools changed our lives, and we believe they can change yours too.

We are genuinely here to help. You can reach out to us directly, anytime. This is Salus — your space to sleep, relax, and restore.
