2. Logo & Icon

The Salus logo is a leaf/droplet icon with a stem motif. Use the SVG wherever possible for crisp rendering.

On dark background
On light background

Logo Usage Rules

  • Minimum clear space around the icon: equal to the icon width on all sides
  • Never stretch, rotate, or alter the icon proportions
  • Use the dark background variant on dark surfaces, transparent on light
  • Minimum display size: 32px
