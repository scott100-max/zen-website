Our Story

salus  /ˈsa.lus/   Latin for health, safety & well-being  ·  Sleep. Relax. Restore.

We're a family. Not a corporation. And this is how Salus came to be.

Salus didn't start in a boardroom. It started in a family living room.

Scott's journey with meditation, stress management, and therapy spans over 40 years — long before mindfulness apps existed, before the iPhone, before "wellness" was an industry. His path has taken him from the temples of Japan to the rice terraces of Bali to the mountains of China, studying with practitioners and teachers along the way. Through decades of personal experience with meditation, ASMR, CBT, counselling, and every relaxation technique imaginable, one thing became clear: these tools genuinely work. But finding them shouldn't require a subscription to a faceless corporation backed by expensive celebrity endorsements.

When our daughter Ella completed her BSc (Hons) and MSc in Psychology, something clicked. We had the lived experience and the clinical expertise under one roof. We could build something different — something that combined decades of personal understanding with rigorous academic knowledge.

That's Salus. A family project built with love, lived experience, and proper psychology. Every session is clinically reviewed. Every technique is evidence-based. And every message you send us is read and answered by a real person.

We're not here to take your money and disappear. We're here because we know what it's like to struggle with anxiety, sleeplessness, and stress — and we know these tools can help, because they helped us first.

What We Stand For

Evidence-Based

Every session is grounded in peer-reviewed research. We work with psychologists to ensure clinical accuracy and safety.

Accessible

Meditation shouldn't be gatekept. We offer free sessions alongside premium content so everyone can start their journey.

Human-First

No gamification, no streaks, no guilt. Salus is designed to support you — not exploit your attention.

Clinical Consultant

Every Salus session is reviewed for psychological safety and clinical accuracy.

“My father spent over 40 years searching for ways to manage stress, anxiety, and sleeplessness — through mindfulness, meditation, cognitive behavioural therapy, breathwork, acupuncture, and every technique he could find. When I completed my psychology degrees, we realised we could combine his lived experience with clinical knowledge to help others. Salus is that vision: evidence-based mindfulness, built by a family who truly understands what it means to struggle — and to heal.”

Ella

BSc (Hons) Psychology · MSc Psychology

Meet Our Founder

The person behind Salus.

Scott

Founder

With over 40 years of personal experience with mindfulness, meditation, cognitive behavioural therapy, counselling, breathwork, visualisation, progressive relaxation, acupuncture, and ASMR — including time spent studying in Japan, Bali, and China — Scott built Salus from lived experience, not a business plan.

Long before mindfulness apps existed, before the iPhone, before "wellness" became an industry — the journey had already begun. Decades of searching, practising, and learning what actually works.

Salus is the result of all that experience, combined with clinical expertise from daughter Ella. A family project, built with love. No expensive celebrities. No venture capital. Just real tools that work, because they worked for us first.

Meet Our Psychologist

The clinical mind behind every session.

Ella

Clinical Consultant · BSc (Hons) · MSc Psychology

Ella is the clinical mind behind Salus. With a BSc (Hons) and MSc in Psychology, she reviews every session for psychological safety, clinical accuracy, and therapeutic integrity.

Growing up watching her father's decades-long journey with meditation and therapy, Ella saw first-hand how these tools could transform someone's life. Her academic training gave her the framework to understand why — and the expertise to ensure Salus does it properly.

She's not just a consultant. She's family. And that's exactly why the science behind Salus is something you can trust.

Meet Our Voice

The person behind every Salus session.

Marco

Narrator & Voice Artist

Marco is the voice you'll hear across every Salus session — from guided meditations and sleep stories to breathing exercises and educational content. His warm, grounded tone has a way of making you feel like you're being guided by someone who genuinely cares.

Over time, we hope you'll come to feel like you know him. That's the point. Salus isn't faceless. It's personal — and Marco is a big part of that.
