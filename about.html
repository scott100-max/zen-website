Our Story

salus  /ˈsa.lus/   health · safety · well-being

Salus was born from a simple belief: everyone deserves access to tools that bring peace, clarity, and balance.

Our Mission

We believe mindfulness shouldn't be complicated. Salus makes meditation accessible, personal, and effective — whether you're a complete beginner or a seasoned practitioner.

Our team of meditation teachers, psychologists, and designers work together to create experiences that genuinely help people feel better, think clearer, and live more intentionally.

Every session is reviewed by our clinical consultant to ensure it meets the highest standards of psychological safety and effectiveness.

Ella Ripley

BSc (Hons) Psychology · MSc Psychology

Ella is the clinical consultant behind every Salus session. With a first-class honours degree and a master's in psychology, she brings rigorous academic knowledge to the practice of mindfulness and meditation.

Her work ensures that Salus content is not only calming and effective but also grounded in the latest psychological research — from cognitive behavioural principles to vagal nerve theory.

Ella specialises in anxiety management, sleep psychology, and evidence-based relaxation techniques.

Our Journey

2023

The Idea

Salus began as a personal project — a search for calm in a noisy world. We realised the tools that helped us could help others too.

2024

Building the Foundation

Partnered with clinical psychologists. Developed our first 50 sessions. Built the platform from the ground up with a focus on simplicity.

2025

Growing the Library

Expanded to 500+ sessions across sleep, focus, stress, and mindfulness. Launched soundscapes, breathing tools, and our education platform.

2026

Where We Are Now

Continuing to grow with new content weekly. Preparing mobile apps for iOS and Android. Exploring partnerships with therapists and workplaces.
