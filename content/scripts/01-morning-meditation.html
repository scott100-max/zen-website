Guided Meditation

10-Minute Morning Meditation

Duration: 10 minutes · Level: All levels · Category: Morning / Focus

Production Notes

Voice: Calm, warm, unhurried. Speak at roughly 120 words per minute. Background: Soft ambient pad in C major, birds at low volume. Visual: Slow sunrise timelapse or soft-focus nature footage.

Intro (0:00–0:30)

[Gentle chime sounds. Ambient music fades in.]

Welcome to Zenscape. This is your ten-minute morning meditation — a gentle way to start your day with clarity and intention.

Find a comfortable position. You can sit upright or lie down — whatever feels right for you this morning. Let your hands rest naturally. Close your eyes when you're ready.

Body Scan & Arrival (0:30–2:30)

Take a deep breath in through your nose... and slowly out through your mouth.

Pause — 5 seconds

Again. Breathe in, filling your lungs completely... and release, letting go of anything you carried from sleep.

Pause — 5 seconds

Begin to notice your body. Feel the weight of your head... your shoulders. Let them soften. Feel the support beneath you — the chair, the floor, the bed. You're held. You're safe.

Scan down through your arms... your hands. Notice any tension and let it dissolve with your next exhale.

Pause — 5 seconds

Move your awareness down through your torso... your hips... your legs... all the way to your feet. Just noticing. No need to change anything.

Breath Awareness (2:30–5:00)

Now bring your attention to your breath. Don't try to control it — just observe. Notice the cool air entering your nostrils... the gentle rise of your chest... the warm air leaving your body.

Pause — 10 seconds

Each breath is a small beginning. Each inhale is an invitation. Each exhale is a release.

If your mind wanders — and it will — that's perfectly natural. Simply notice where it went, and gently guide your attention back to the breath. No judgment. Just returning.

Pause — 30 seconds (breathing space)

You're doing beautifully. Stay with the breath.

Pause — 30 seconds

Setting Intention (5:00–7:00)

Now, keeping your eyes closed, I'd like you to think about the day ahead. Not the tasks or the schedule — just the feeling you want to carry with you.

Pause — 5 seconds

Maybe it's calm. Maybe it's focus. Maybe it's kindness — toward yourself and others. Choose one word. One intention. Let it settle in your mind like a stone in still water.

Pause — 10 seconds

Hold that word gently. Don't grip it — just let it be there. This is your anchor for the day. When things feel rushed or uncertain, you can return to this word. It's always there for you.

Pause — 15 seconds

Gratitude (7:00–8:30)

Before we begin to close, take a moment to feel grateful. Not for anything specific — though you can if you'd like — but simply for this moment. For the fact that you chose to be here, to begin your day with stillness.

Pause — 10 seconds

Gratitude has a way of softening us. It opens a small door that lets light in. Even on difficult days, this moment is yours.

Pause — 10 seconds

Closing (8:30–10:00)

Now, gently begin to bring your awareness back to the room. Feel the surface beneath you. Notice any sounds around you — the hum of the morning.

Wiggle your fingers and toes. Take one more deep breath in... and a long, slow exhale.

Pause — 5 seconds

When you're ready, open your eyes. Take in the light. You've given yourself a gift this morning — ten minutes of stillness that will carry through the hours ahead.

Thank you for practising with Zenscape. Sleep. Relax. Restore.

[Music fades out over 10 seconds. End with Zenscape chime.]
