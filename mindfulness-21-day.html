21-DAY PROGRAMME

Transform Your Mind

Three weeks to build a lasting mindfulness practice. Go deeper than you ever have before.

What You'll Learn

This isn't just a collection of meditations — it's a structured journey. Each week builds on the last, taking you from foundational awareness to advanced practices that will stay with you for life.

Week 1: Foundation

Breath awareness, body scanning, and the basics of present-moment attention.

Week 2: Expansion

Emotional awareness, thought observation, and loving-kindness practices.

Week 3: Integration

Open awareness, daily life integration, and building your ongoing practice.

Day 1: The Breath

12 minutes · Breath awareness · Your foundation

Everything begins with the breath. Today you'll learn to use your breath as an anchor — a place to return to whenever your mind wanders. This single skill is the foundation of every mindfulness practice that follows.

Day 2: Body Awareness

12 minutes · Body scan · Connecting mind and body

Learn to scan your body from head to toe, noticing sensations without trying to change them. This practice builds the interoceptive awareness that helps you recognise stress before it overwhelms you.
