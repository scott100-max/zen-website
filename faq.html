Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Salus.

Is Salus free?
Yes! Salus offers a free tier with sample meditations, the breathing timer, and daily mindfulness tips. Premium unlocks the full library and sleep stories for £5.99/month or £49.99/year (save 30%).
How does the subscription work?
You get Subscribe monthly or yearly for full Premium access. Cancel anytime. You can also use the free tier with limited sessions.
Can I cancel my subscription?
Absolutely. You can cancel anytime from your account settings. You'll keep Premium access until the end of your current billing period.
What devices does Salus work on?
Salus is currently available on the web, accessible from any device with a browser. iOS and Android apps are coming soon.
I'm new to meditation. Where should I start?
Try our 10-Minute Morning Meditation — it's a gentle introduction to breath awareness and body scanning. The breathing timer is also a great starting point for beginners.
How do I contact support?
Visit our contact page to send us a message. We typically respond within 24 hours.
